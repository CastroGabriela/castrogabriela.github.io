Design System

Logo

The logo is a wordmark that contains the name of its founder. The bold san serif font wants to communicate a message of professionalism and openness, evoking a feeling of confidence and trust in their energy partners.

The logos are for editorial use only in newspapers, news magazines, trade publications, broadcast media and online media. All logos remain the property of Hess Corporation and are federally registered trademarks of Hess.

The logos cannot be edited or altered in any manner or used for any personal or commercial purpose. Do not redraw or alter the placement, size relationships, colors or typeface of the Hess logo's elements.

Fonts: Krunch Bold + Nunito Extra Light

Clearspace

The clearspace of the logo must be 50px. The distance each letter is 10px. No graphic element should be in this zone.

*Always use the logo files provided. Do not re-create.
