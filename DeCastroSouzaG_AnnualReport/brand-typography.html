TYPOGRAPHY USE

In general, Montserrat Extra Bold should be reserved for large, bold type treatments and headlines to create impact.

Montserrat Semi Bold may also be used for titles and medium-sized headlines.

Montserrat Light Italic is a title support typeface and should be used to emphasize a word.

Montserrat Regular is the primary typeface for body copy.
