COLOR PALETTE

Hess Corporation primary color palette is the Pantone 555C. The other colors are secondary.

The green represents the environment (oil and natural gas), balance, reliability, stability, and safety.

The yellow represents energy, opportunity, confidence, and success.

Primary Color Usage

White text in green background only. The word CORPORATION is always white or black. White in color background; black in white background.

Secondary Color Usage

White text in light yellow is not allowed to keep contrast.

Secondary Color Usage

Green text in dark yellow is not allowed to keep contrast.
